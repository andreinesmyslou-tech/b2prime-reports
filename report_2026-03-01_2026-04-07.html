🏆 Top Converter: 45-54
CR KYC→Deposit 75% — 48pp above industry benchmark (27%). Contributes 14% of all deposits this period. Average deposit $2,760 vs. industry benchmark $7,500.
Why: Highest avg deposit in industry. Slow decision cycle — 3-7 days from KYC to deposit vs. 1-2 days for younger groups. Personal account manager call increases CR by 40-60%. Avg LTV 24-36 months.
Action: 45+ is your highest average deposit segment with a slow 3-7 day decision cycle — the post-KYC screen lacking a Deposit CTA (PAN-41399) is especially damaging here. They will not search for the button. Required actions: (1) Account manager personal call within 24h of KYC — increases CR by 40-60% for this demographic. (2) Prepare a premium PDF onboarding pack: B2Prime regulatory credentials (FCA/CySEC), fund safety (segregated accounts via Praxis gateway), step-by-step deposit guide with bank wire and SEPA instructions. (3) 2FA is critical — 45+ users need to know withdrawal is always possible; include Authenticator setup guide in the onboarding pack.
